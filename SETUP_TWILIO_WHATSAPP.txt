================================================================================
  PASUL 1: CREEAZA CONT TWILIO
================================================================================

1. Mergi la: https://www.twilio.com/try-twilio

2. Click pe "Sign Up" sau "Try Twilio Free"

3. Completeaza:
   - Nume
   - Email
   - Parola
   - Verifica email-ul

4. Introdu numarul de telefon pentru verificare

5. Alege tipul de utilizare:
   - Selecteaza "Explore Twilio" sau "Build an app"

6. Completeaza profilul

7. VERIFICA EMAIL-UL pentru activare!

8. DUPACEA dai "Verify Phone Number" pentru telefon

9. MERGI LA CONSOLE: https://console.twilio.com/

================================================================================
  PASUL 2: OBTINE CREDENTIALELE
================================================================================

IN TWILIO CONSOLE:

1. Account SID:
   - Mergi la: https://console.twilio.com/
   - Account SID se afla in coltul de sus dreapta
   - Exemple: ACxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx

2. Auth Token:
   - Click pe "View" sau "Show"
   - Copy Auth Token
   - Exemplu: abc123def456ghi789jkl012mno345

3. Twilio Phone Number:
   - Mergi la: https://console.twilio.com/us1/develop/phone-numbers/manage/incoming
   - Sau: https://console.twilio.com/us1/develop/phone-numbers/manage/search
   - Ia un numar (FREE pentru test)
   - Exemplu: +1234567890

================================================================================
  PASUL 3: CONFIGUREAZA WHATSAPP
================================================================================

IN TWILIO CONSOLE:

1. Mergi la: https://console.twilio.com/us1/develop/sms/overview

2. Click pe "Get Started with WhatsApp"

3. Sau mergi direct la:
   https://console.twilio.com/us1/develop/sms/try-it-out/whatsapp-learn

4. Configureaza Sandbox:
   - Twilio va-ti da un cod pentru Sandbox
   - Trimite acel cod pe WhatsApp
   - Confirma pe WhatsApp

5. NOW YOU CAN SEND WHATSAPP MESSAGES!

================================================================================
  PASUL 4: CONFIGUREAZA PE SERVER
================================================================================

ACUM intruneste toate datele:

1. Account SID: _______________________________
2. Auth Token: _______________________________
3. Twilio Number: ____________________________
4. NumÄƒrul tau (WhatsApp): ____________________

ACUM CONFIGUREAZA PE SERVER:

ssh root@185.182.121.45

cat > /usr/local/bin/whatsapp_send.sh <<'EOFTWILIO'
#!/bin/bash
ACCOUNT_SID="PASTE_AICI_ACCOUNT_SID"
AUTH_TOKEN="PASTE_AICI_AUTH_TOKEN"
TWILIO_NUM="PASTE_AICI_TWILIO_NUMBER"
RECIPIENT="PASTE_AICI_NUMARUL_TAU_WHATSAPP"

message="$1"

curl -s -X POST "https://api.twilio.com/2010-04-01/Accounts/${ACCOUNT_SID}/Messages.json" \
  --data-urlencode "From=whatsapp:${TWILIO_NUM}" \
  --data-urlencode "To=whatsapp:${RECIPIENT}" \
  --data-urlencode "Body=${message}" \
  -u "${ACCOUNT_SID}:${AUTH_TOKEN}"

echo "WhatsApp message sent!"
EOFTWILIO

chmod +x /usr/local/bin/whatsapp_send.sh

# TEST
/usr/local/bin/whatsapp_send.sh "Test WhatsApp de pe server!"

# VERIFICA TELEFONUL!
